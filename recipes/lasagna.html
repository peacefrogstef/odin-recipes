Lasagna with cheese and ground meat

Description

While there are a few steps to this lasagna recipe, itâ€™s easy to make and has so much flavor. This dish can be made ahead of time and freezes well either before or after baking!

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Lasagna dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Stir fry the beef mince
  2. Place lasagna dough in hot water for 5 minutes
  3. Add tomato sauce while stir frying the beef
  4. Place everything in the oven and bake for 30 minutes
  5. When done, grind cheese on top
