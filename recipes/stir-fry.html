Beef stir fry

Description

Easy Beef Stir Fry is the perfect Chinese-restaurant-inspired dish that you can make at home. To get delicious flavor use lots of been, your favorite vegetables, and of course a great sauce.

Ingredients

  • Diced Beef
  • Brocoli and bell peper
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice noodles

Steps

  1. Stir fry the beef
  2. Add soy sauce
  3. Add brocoli and bell peper
  4. Season with peper and salt
  5. Boil rice noodles and add them to the stir fry
