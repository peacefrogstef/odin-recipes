Turkey soup

Description

A turkey carcass soup is the answer when you don't want to waste a single bite of turkey. This is a delicious soup and one of my favorites. If you want to freeze some, leave out the potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 turkey carcass
  • 6 small potatoes, diced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • Â¼ teaspoon ground paprika

Steps

  1. Place turkey carcass into a large soup pot; pour in water and bring to a boil over medium heat
  2. Remove turkey carcass from the pot. Remove and chop any remaining turkey meat.
  3. Strain broth through a fine mesh strainer into a clean soup pot
  4. Reduce heat and stir
  5. Remove bay leaf before serving
